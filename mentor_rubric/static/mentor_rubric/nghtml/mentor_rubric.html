Mentor takes a strengths-based stance regarding their mentee.

1

Not Meeting Expectations

Complains about mentee without an effort to maintain positivity about mentee potential. Makes negative assumptions about mentee motivations or behavior without sufficient information.
2

Meeting Expectations

Mentor makes an effort to identify and capitalize on the strengths of the mentee regardless of the context and demonstrates persistence and positivity in the face of challenging situations with their mentee.
3

Exceeding Expectations

Mentor demonstrates an extraordinary ability to identify and capitalize on the strengths of the mentee regardless of the context and demonstrates persistence and positivity in the face of challenging situations with their mentee.

Mentor supports mentee in utilizing all resources, when appropriate (including reaching out to their staff member or other external resources).

1

Not Meeting Expectations

When opportunities to identify additional support for a mentee emerge, mentor does not utilize appropriate resources.
2

Meeting Expectations

When appropriate, mentor identifies additional support structures for the mentee based on mentee’s need (e.g. family, guidance counselor, etc.).
3

Exceeding Expectations

Mentor takes initiative beyond locating an individual to help his/her mentee and identifies resources based on a mentee’s need and specifically aids in the mentee’s addressing challenges or answering difficult questions.

Mentor responds to staff member in a timely fashion.

1

Not Meeting Expectations

Mentor consistently does not respond to staff member within a week of being contacted.
2

Meeting Expectations

Mentor, on average, responds to staff member within a week of being contacted for any programmatic reasons.
3

Exceeding Expectations

Mentor consistently responds to any staff member contacts within 48 hours.

Mentor models effective use of canvas prompt curriculum.

1

Not Meeting Expectations

Mentor consistently does not adhere to the canvas prompt guidelines.
2

Meeting Expectations

Mentor adheres to canvas prompt guidelines on a regular basis and communicates in an appropriate way.
3

Exceeding Expectations

Mentor includes more high-quality and informative detail in his/her communications to his/her mentee than is suggested by the canvas prompt guidelines.
